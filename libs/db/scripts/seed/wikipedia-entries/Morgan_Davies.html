Morgan Davies

Australian film and television actor

Morgan Davies (born 2001 or 2002) is an Australian actor.

Early and personal life

Davies grew up in Sydney's suburb Rozelle with a single mother. He first came out as transgender to his mother and close friends at the age of 13 and came out publicly in 2020. Davies has become an advocate for transgender rights and has publicly talked about his battles with depression as a teenager and his stage fright.

Career

Davies made his film debut as child actor in the Australian film The Tree.

He played Madeline in Storm Boy alongside Geoffrey Rush and Jai Courtney.

He starred in the 2020 television series The End as a trans teen in the process of gender transition.

Davies portrayed one of the main characters, the teenage boy Danny, in Evil Dead Rise, the 2023 instalment of the Evil Dead franchise.
